Ingredients

  • Plantain
  • oil
  • ginger
  • ground pepper

    • Steps

    1. Cut the Plantain into smaller pieces
    2. add oil to the Plantain
    3. blend the ginger and add to the Plantain
    4. add the ground pepper
    5. fry in a pan for about 15 minutes, turing the Plantain every five minutes
